DABELI RECIPE

Dabeli Recipe

Prep in: 15m

Cooks in: 20m

Total in: 30m

Makes: 1 Serve

Ingredients

  • 1 pav
  • 3/4 teaspoon butter
  • 1 and 1/2 teaspoon sev
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic mayonnaise
  • 1 and 1/2 teaspoon pomergranate
  • 1/4 clove
  • 1/4 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 1/4 potato
  • 1/4 teaspoon water
  • salt As required e
  • 1/4 handful coriander leaves
  • 1 and 1/2 teaspoon raw peanuts
  • 1/4 onion
  • 3/4 tablespoon tamrind chutney
  • 1/4 teaspoon coriander seeds
  • 1/4 red chilli
  • cinnamon stick as required
  • asafoetida as required
  • 3/4 tablespoon green chutney
  • 1/4 teaspoon cumin seeds

How to make Dabeli

Step 1 Prepare the Dabeli masala and boil the potatoes for filling

Firstly, place a saucepan over medium flame and roast clove, cinnamon stick, coriander seeds and red chilli in it for about two minutes. On the other hand, boil the potatoes in a pressure cooker over medium flame and then cool them. Now, peel the potatoes and mash them in a bowl. Put the roasted masala mixture in a grinder and grind finely. Now, on a chopping board, chop the onions finely.

Step 2 Make the filling for Dabeli using mashed potatoes

For the filling, place another pan over medium flame and heat oil in it. Now, add the cumin seeds in it. Let these crackle and add asafoetida, ground masala, mashed potato, water and salt in the mixture, and mix well. Now, remove the pan from the flame, and add tamarind chutney in it. Mix well all the ingredients.

Step 3 Roast the pav using a little butter

Now, take the pav and cut them into halves. Place a pan over medium flame and melt butter in it. Now, place the sliced pav on it and cook them on both sides. Once the bun is cooked from both sides, take them off the flame and place them on a flat surface.

Step 4 Add the potato filling in the roasted pav and cover with another

Next, put a portion of the filling on the lower half of each pav. Top it with the chopped onion, sev, coriander leaves, pomegranate, peanuts, 5 tablespoons of green chutney and garlic mayonnaise. Cover it with another half of the pav. Prepare the remaining dabeli in the same manner and drizzle 1 tablespoon green chutney over the dabeli. Serve it warm to enjoy!

Step 5

Take out the paneer curry in a bowl and garnish with coriander. Serve hot with chapati and rice.
