KACHUMBER RECIPE

Kachumber Salad Recipe

Prep in: 05m

Cooks in: 00m

Total in: 10m

Makes: 1 Serve

Ingredients

  • 1/4 large cucumber
  • 1/4 medium tomato
  • black pepper As required
  • 1/2 tablespoon coriander leaves
  • 1/4 small onion
  • black salt As required
  • 1/4 tablespoon lemon juice
  • 1/4 teaspoon cumin powder

How to make Kachumber Salad

Step 1 Mix the ingredients.

In a bowl, add chopped cucumber, onion and tomato. Also add roasted cumin powder, lemon juice and adjust black salt as per taste. Mix the ingredients well.

Step 2 Ready to be served.

Transfer the salad to a serving bowl. Garnish the kachumber salad with chopped coriander leaves along with black pepper and serve.
