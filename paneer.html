Paneer Makhanwala Recipe

Prep in: 10m

Cooks in: 20m

Total in: 30m

Makes: 1 Serve

Ingredients

  • 1/3 cup and 1 and 1/4 tablespoon paneer
  • 1/4 teaspoon garam masala powder
  • 1 tablespoon butter
  • 1/4 cup and 1 and 1/4 teaspoon tomato puree
  • 1/4 teaspoon red chilli powder
  • 3/4 green chilli
  • 1/4 cup and 1 and 1/4 tablespoon fresh cream
  • 2/3 tablespoon dried fenugreek leaves
  • salt As required

How to make Paneer Makhanwala

Step 1

To start with, wash paneer and chop it properly into small cubes on a chopping board. Chop the green chilies as well. Take a pan and keep it on medium flame. And butter to the pan and let it melt, it won't take much time to melt.

Step 2

After the butter melts, add green chilies, tomato puree, kasuri methi, salt, red chili powder to the pan. Stir well to let the ingredients mix.

Step 3

Cover the pan or you can also keep it uncovered and simmer for 7-8 minutes by reducing the flame to low or otherwise your spices can burn.

Step 4

Then add cream and garam masala to the pan. Let the mixture cook for another 5 minutes on medium flame. Once the mixture is cooked properly, finally add paneer cubes to the pan and cook for another 2 minutes.

Step 5

Take out the paneer curry in a bowl and garnish with coriander. Serve hot with chapati and rice.

Paneer Cutlet Recipe

Prep in: 20m

Cooks in: 20m

Total in: 40m

Makes: 1 Serve

Ingredients

  • 1/3 cup paneer
  • 1/2 green chilli
  • 1/4 handful chopped coriander leaves
  • 1/4 teaspoon red chilli powder
  • 1/3 cup crushed breadcrumbs
  • 1 pinches powdered salt
  • 2 teaspoon Water
  • 1/4 cup and 1 and 1/4 tablespoon potato
  • 1 tablespoon all purpose flour
  • 1/4 teaspoon garam masala powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon lemon juice
  • 2 and 3/4 tablespoon Refined oil
  • 1/4 handful chopped fenugreek leaves ( methi)

How to make Paneer Cutlet

Step 1

To make these yummy Paneer cutlets, first was the paneer nicely. Then crush it and keep it in a bowl for some time.

Step 2

Take the potatoes, boil them and mash them nicely. Take a big bowl and put the crushed panner, 2 tablespoon all purpose flour, mashed potatoes, red chilli powder, lemon juice, salt, chopped fenugreek leaves and garam masala in it. Mix well

Step 3

Then add some water and 2 table spoon of all purpose flour and mix again. Make sure there are no lumps left.

Step 4

Form this into balls of small size. Then flatten them with a light hand. Take the breadcrumbs and some all purpose flour and put them on 2 separate plates.

Step 5

Take a pan and heat oil in it for frying. Take the flattened cutlet balls and coat them in all purpose flour first and then breadcrumbs.

Step 6

Put them in the hot oil and fry until the cutlets turn golden brown in colour.

Step 7

Your Paneer Cutlets are ready to be served.

Paneer Kheer Recipe

Prep in: 05m

Cooks in: 15m

Total in: 20m

Makes: 1 Serve

Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup grated paneer
  • 1/4 teaspoon powdered green cardamom
  • 3/4 cup milk
  • 2 tablespoon sugar

For Garnishing

  • almonds As required
  • pistachios As required

How to make Paneer Cutlet

Step 1

To prepare this yummy dish, first of all grate the paneer in a bowl and keep aside.

Step 2

Now, put a deep-bottomed pan on medium flame and add milk in it. Bring milk to a boil and then add grated paneer in it, mix well and let it simmer for about 10 minutes, stirring continuously till it thickens.

Step 3

When the kheer thickens, sprinkle the cardamom powder and add sugar in the pan. Stir to mix well and cook for abot two minutes for mixing the sugar. Once done, turn off the burner and garnish with almonds and pistachios. Serve hot and enjoy! (Note: You can add more your choice of nuts and raisins to the kheer).
