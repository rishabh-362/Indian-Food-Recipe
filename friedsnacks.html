Samosa Recipe

Prep in: 15m

Cooks in: 40m

Total in: 55m

Makes: 1 Serve

Ingredients

  • 1/3 cup and 2 and 1/2 tablespoon all purpose flour
  • 3/4 potato
  • salt As required
  • turmeric as required
  • garam masala powder as required
  • sugar as required
  • carom seeds as required
  • 1 and 1/2 tablespoon peas
  • red chilli powder as required
  • kasoori methi powder as required
  • chaat masala powder as required

For The Main Dish

  • 3/4 tablespoon ghee

How to make Samosa

Step 1 Knead the dough

To prepare this easy recipe, take a dough kneading plate and add all-purpose flour in it. Add a little salt and water and knead a fine dough of it. Once done, keep the dough aside to rest for 30 minutes.

Step 2 Prepare the potato filling

Now, put a kadhai over medium flame and heat a little oil in it. When the oil is hot enough, add boiled & mashed potatoes along with all the spices - red chilli powder, turmeric, salt to taste, kasoori methi powder, garam masala powder, chaat masala powder and a little sugar. Mix well and then add peas in it. Stir fry for 2-3 minutes. Once done, transfer to a bowl and keep aside until required.

Step 3 Make roti and cut into a triangular shape

Pinch a little dough and roll it into a thin roti. Cut this into half and fold into a cone-like shape. Add potato masala in this cone and seal it using a little water. Let the samosas dry for 30 minutes.

Step 4 Fry the samosas

Once dried, put a kadhai over medium flame and when the oil is hot enough, fry the samosas for till golden brown and properly fried. Serve with tamarind and green chutney.

Dal Kachori Recipe

Prep in: 12m

Cooks in: 15m

Total in: 27m

Makes: 1 Serve

Ingredients

  • 2 and 3/4 tablespoon wheat flour
  • salt as required
  • 1/4 teaspoon kasoori methi powder
  • 1/3 teaspoon carom seeds
  • 1/3 teaspoon ghee
  • 1/3 cup refined oil
  • 1/4 teaspoon cumin powder
  • baking soda as required
  • water as required

For Filling

  • 1/4 tablespoon urad dal
  • 1/4 teaspoon fennel seeds powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon coriander powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon red chilli powder

How to make Dal Kachori

Step 1 Knead a soft dough of wheat flour

Dal Kachori is one of the most popular dishes in Rajasthan, which can be prepared at home using some easily available ingredients. Take a large bowl, add wheat flour, carom seeds, salt to taste and 2 tsp ghee. Mix well use water to knead a soft dough. Cover the dough and keep it aside for 15 to 20 minutes. Now, wash and soak urad dal for 10 minutes.

Step 2 Saute spices and add soaked dal

Take the soaked dal and grind to form a fine paste. Put a pan over medium flame and add 1 tsp oil in it. Add carom seeds, soaked urad dal paste, red chilli powder, coriander powder, fennel seeds, cumin powder, salt to taste, kasoori methi powder and baking soda. Stir well and add a little water to mix the spices. Once done, turn off the flame and allow it to cool down.

Step 3 Make kachoris and shallow fry

Now, take one dough ball and flatten it with the help of your fingers. Fill it with dal stuffing and make kachori. Repeat the process with other dough balls. Meanwhile, heat oil in a pan and once it's hot enough, deep fry the kachoris till they turn golden brown. Your crunchy Dal Kachoris are ready to be served. It tastes best when served with green coriander chutney or sweet chutney.

Onion Pakoda Recipe

Prep in: 10m

Cooks in: 10m

Total in: 20m

Makes: 1 Serve

Ingredients

  • 1/3 cup and 2 teaspoon onion
  • 1/4 tablespoon rice flour
  • 1/4 teaspoon garlic paste
  • 1/4 teaspoon dry mango powder
  • salt as required
  • water as required
  • 1/4 cup gram flour (besan)
  • 1/4 teaspoon ginger
  • 1/4 teaspoon carom seeds
  • 1/4 teaspoon green chilli
  • 1/4 cup vegetable oil

How to make Onion Pakoda

Step 1 Prepare the batter

Take a big bowl, add besan, flour, salt and carrom seeds in it. Add water in batches and mix well to form a thick batter.

Step 2 Add onions

Now add chopped green chillies, grated ginger, garlic paste, dry mango powder and mix well. Add thinly sliced onions in the batter, add more water and mix. Your batter should be neither too thick nor too thin.

Step 3 Heat the oil and deep fry

Put the kadhai on medium heat and let it heat properly. Now drop in the pakodas one by one in the hot oil.

Step 4 Fry and serve

Deep fry all the pakodas until golden brown and serve wi ketchup or mint chutney. Do try this recipe, rate it and let us know how it turned out to be.
