Idli Sambhar Recipe

Prep in: 15m

Cooks in: 30m

Total in: 45m

Makes: 1 Serve

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup rice
  • 1/4 tablespoon fenugreek seeds
  • 1/4 tablespoon baking soda
  • 1/4 cup toor dal
  • 1/2 tablespoon chilli powder
  • 1/4 tablespoon cumin powder
  • 1/2 tablespoon tamarind
  • asafoetida as required
  • 1/4 tablespoon cumin seeds
  • 1/4 tablespoon jaggery
  • 1/4 cup urad dal
  • 1/4 teaspoon granulated sugar
  • 1/4 tablespoon salt
  • 3 teaspoon refined oil
  • 1/4 tablespoon turmeric
  • 1/4 tablespoon garam masala powder
  • 1/2 tablespoon coriander leaves
  • 1 and 1/4 cloves garlic
  • 1/4 tablespoon mustard seeds

How to make Idli Sambhar

Step 1

To prepare this easy recipe, take a large bowl and wash rice and urad dal together in it. Soak them in water for 8-10 hours or overnight! In the morning, grind the mixture to make a fine batter, transfer to a large bowl and keep it covered for a night. In the morning, add fenugreek seeds, salt, baking soda, sugar in the mixture and mix once again.

Step 2

Add some salt to the idli batter and stir well, keep aside until required. Meanwhile, make the preparation of Sambhar. For the same, add the toor dal in a pressure cooker with enough water and cook for 2-3 whistles. Once done, add the idli batter in the mould and cook for 10 minutes. Take all ingredients ready for sambhar in the big thali. Take the cooked dal, add turmeric powder in it along with salt and sugar. Mix well and blend the dal with a wire gauze.

Step 3

Add hot water to the mixture and stir well. Now, put a pan over medium flame and heat a little oil in it. Once the oil is hot enough, add cumin seeds in it and saute for a few seconds. Next, add mustard seeds, asafoetida in it and saute for another few seconds. Finally, add garlic paste and saute until the raw smell goes away. Then, add tomatoes in it and let the mixture fry for 2 minutes. Now, add chilli powder and Maggi masala along with tamarind juice in the pan, and stir well. Let the mixture cook for another 2 minutes and then add the cooked liquid dal to it. Add the blended dal in the pan and let it boil for a few minutes. Your tasty Sambhar is ready to serve.

Rava Uttapam Recipe

Prep in: 10m

Cooks in: 25m

Total in: 35m

Makes: 1 Serve

Ingredients

  • 1/3 cup semolina
  • 3/4 small onion
  • 1/4 teaspoon baking soda
  • salt as required
  • refined oil as required
  • 3/4 small tomato
  • 1/3 cup water
  • 2 teaspoon lemon juice
  • 3/4 green chillies

How to make Rava Uttapam

Step 1

To start with, take a deep bowl and combine the semolina and water. Mix them into a smooth batter and keep it aside for at least 40 minutes. Once done, stir and mix the batter again and then add salt, baking soda and lemon juice. Mix the batter well and ensure no lumps are formed in the batter.

Step 2

Now, heat oil in a non-stick pan and pour a small portion of batter on it. Spread the portion in an concentric circle and then add thinly sliced onions, thinly sliced tomato and sliced green chillies on the the uttapam batter (ensure that onions, tomatoes and green chillies stick to the batter).

Step 3

Now, drizzle some oil on the batter and also on the sides of the pan. Cook the uttapam till it is brown from the surface and then flip it to cook the other side as well. Cook it till the onions are brown and then transfer it to the plate. Serve it warm with your favourite chutney.

Semolina Payasam Recipe

Prep in: 10m

Cooks in: 15m

Total in: 25m

Makes: 1 Serve

Ingredients

  • 2 cup milk
  • sugar as required
  • 1 tablespoon ghee
  • 3 tablespoon semolina
  • 1 teaspoon green cardamom

For Garnishing

  • 2 tablespoon roasted cashews

How to make Paneer Cutlet

Step 1

Heat ghee over low-medium flame and fry the cashew nuts till golden brown in colour and keep it aside.

Step 2

Boil milk over low-medium flame in a thick-bottomed pan. Meanwhile, heat a kadhai and roast the semolina (rava) in little ghee till the raw smell disappears.

Step 3

Add this roasted rava to the boiled milk and stir well. Then, add sugar as required along with cardamom powder. Mix well on low flame till the rava gets cooked.

Step 4

When the rava is cooked completely, garnish the dessert with roasted cashews and serve hot. Enjoy!
