SHRIMP PAKORA RECIPE

Shrimp Pakora Recipe

Prep in: 10m

Cooks in: 20m

Total in: 30m

Makes: 1 Serve

Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup and 3 teaspoon shrimps
  • 1 pinches red chilli powder
  • 1 tablespoon gram flour (besan)
  • 1 and 1/2 cloves garlic
  • 1/4 teaspoon lemon juice
  • 1/4 cup refined oil
  • 1/4 onion
  • 1/2 green chilli
  • 1/2 teaspoon rice flour
  • 1/2 handful coriander leaves
  • salt as required

How to make Shrimp Pakora

Step 1

To make this easy snack recipe, wash the shrimps under cold running water and then pat dry them. Once they are properly dried, transfer them to a glass bowl. Then add the gram flour, rice flour, garlic, onions, red chilli powder, green chillies, coriander leaves and salt in the same bowl.

Step 2

Mix all the ingredients mentioned in the previous step properly. Then add lemon juice and 1 tbsp of oil in the mixture. Mix well again and then using your hands, shape the mixture into cutlets or any other shape of your choice. Meanwhile, add oil in a deep nonstick pan and heat it over moderate flame.

Step 3

Now, once the oil is sufficiently hot, carefully place the prepared pakoras in the frying pan and allow it to fry until it gets a golden brown colour. Once done, remove and drain the excess oil and transfer the pakoras in a serving plate. Serve it with chutney of your choice. You can also pair it with a cocktail or mocktail.
