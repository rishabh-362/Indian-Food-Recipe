MUTTON CHAAP RECIPE

Mutton Chaap Recipe

Prep in: 10m

Cooks in: 45m

Total in: 55m

Makes: 1 Serve

Ingredients

  • 1/3 cup and 1 and 1/4 tablespoon mutton ribs
  • 1/4 teaspoon ginger paste
  • 1/4 black cardamom
  • 1/4 cinnamon stick
  • clove as required
  • spice chili powder As required
  • 1/4 teaspoon cumin powder
  • 1 and 1/4 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • salt As required
  • 3/4 onion
  • 1/3 teaspoon garlic paste
  • water As required
  • 1/3 teaspoon red chilli powder
  • mace as required
  • 1/2 peppercorns
  • yoghurt (curd) As required

How to make Mutton Chaap

Step 1 Marinate the mutton ribs

Wash the mutton ribs properly and gently then pat dry them using a kitchen towel. Take a large bowl and add curd, mace powder, ginger-garlic paste and salt to it. Mix all the ingredients well and marinate the mutton ribs in it. Refrigerate for some time.

Step 2 Saute onions & spices

Place a pressure cooker over medium flame and pour oil in it. Once the oil is hot enough add sliced onions to it. Saute the onions well till they turn translucent. Now add peppercorns, water, cloves, red chilli powder, spice chilli powder, turmeric powder, cumin powder, crushed black cardamom and salt to it. Mix well.

Step 3 Add the marinated mutton & pressure cook

Now, add the marinated mutton ribs into the pressure cooker and stir fry for minimum 20 minutes over medium flame. Cover the pressure cooker with a lid and cook till 6 whistles over medium flame.

Step 4 Serve your homemade Mutton Chaap!

Your mutton chaap is now ready to be served. Serve hot with steamed rice or chapatis and enjoy.
