PARDEWAALI MURGH HAWAH MAHAL RECIPE

Pardewaali Murgh Hawah Mahal Recipe

Prep in: 10m

Cooks in: 30m

Total in: 40m

Makes: 1 Serve

Ingredients

  • 180 gm chicken
  • 10 gm garlic
  • 5 gm gram flour (besan)
  • salt as required
  • 20 gm grated cheese cubes
  • 30 gm butter
  • 5 gm green chilli
  • 1 egg
  • 1 teaspoon black pepper

How to make Pardewaali Murgh Hawah Mahal

Step 1

To make this lip-smacking appetizer recipe, place a pan over medium flame and add butter in it. Once melted, add garlic into the pan and saute it well until the raw smell goes away.

Step 2

After about 30 seconds, add the chicken and green chili in the pan and season with salt and black pepper powder.

Step 3

Once the chicken is halfway through, add besan in it and cook for a while.

Step 4

Next, put the chicken into seekh and cook for another 3 minutes.

Step 5

Meanwhile, beat egg in a bowl and then add the grated cheese. Mix well.

Step 5

Now, dip the chicken in the egg-cheese mixture and cook in tandoor till cooked thoroughly.

Step 7

Once done, your Pardewaali Murgh Hawah Mahal is ready. Serve hot with mint and peanut chutney along with spring mix.
