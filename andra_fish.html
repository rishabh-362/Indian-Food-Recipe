ANDRA FISH RECIPE

Andhra Fish Fry (Andhra Chepala Vepudu) Recipe

Prep in: 5m

Cooks in: 20m

Total in: 25m

Makes: 1 Serve

Ingredients

  • 1 pieces Fish
  • 0.5 tsp Ginger Garlic paste
  • 0.5 tsp Chilli Powder
  • 0.25 tsp Turmeric
  • 0.5 tsp Salt
  • 0.5 tbsp Oil
  • 0.5 tsp Lemon Juice (optional)

How to make Andhra Fish Fry

Step 1

Firstly clean and wash the fish pieces. If required, add little rock salt and curd to the fish pieces and wash.

Step 2

Take ginger garlic paste, chilli powder, turmeric and salt in a vessel. Mix well and coat the masala over the fish pieces and set aside for 5-10mins.

Step 3

After 10mins, place the vessel over the stove and cook the fish for 3-4 mins by covering the vessel with a lid.

Step 4

After a minute or two, flip over the fish pieces and let it cook for a minute or more and switch off the flame.

Step 5

Meanwhile, heat oil in a frying pan. Here I am frying fish with less oil, if you want to deep fry then add more oil.

Step 6

When the oil is hot enough, place the fish pieces and frying them on a medium flame for 3-4 mins.

Step 7

After 3mins, flip the fish pieces and fry till golden brown on both sides.

Step 8

Fish fry is ready to serve. Transfer the fish piece to a serving plate and add lemon juice before serving.
